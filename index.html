About me

I am a trainer and teacher of theatrical improvisation and an expert in agile methodologies with more than thirty years of experience in software developing.

I work with very different groups: corporate teams, artists, students, people with no theatrical background at all.

I collaborate with an international network of professionals and training organizations, integrating artistic, pedagogical, and organizational expertise.
